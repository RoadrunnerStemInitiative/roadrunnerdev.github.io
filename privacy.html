Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2025

Roadrunner STEM Initiative respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with our forms or contact us:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Mailing address (optional)
  • Resume link or experience details

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Follow up on board interest or volunteer inquiries
  • Gather community feedback via surveys
  • Communicate about events, opportunities, or resources related to our mission

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share it if required by law.

Form Submissions

Form submissions are handled via Formspree. Your data is processed securely and only sent to Roadrunner STEM Initiative staff.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking scripts on our website at this time.

Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data by contacting us.
